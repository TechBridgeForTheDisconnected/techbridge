Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fix a slow device?

Close unused applications and clear cache to speed up your device.

Why does my WiFi keep disconnecting?

Forget the network and reconnect, or restart your router.

What should I do if my battery drains quickly?

Reduce screen brightness, close background apps, and disable location services.

How can I fix software that's not responding?

Try updating or reinstalling the software, and restart your device.

What should I do if I have no internet connection?

Try restarting your router or checking with your ISP.

About Us

We are a group of students from Hericks High School, and our goal is to serve our community. This website is the vehicle through which we serve, designed to help those struggling to keep up with the ever-evolving world of technology. We understand how difficult it is to stay up-to-date, especially for seniors and individuals with visual impairments. Our story began when one of our group members visited a relative in the hospital. During their conversation, the relative expressed her biggest issue: the inability to connect with her family. This inspired us as a group to develop a tool that could help anyone meet their needs. We strive to help and serve in ways that improve our community and the world at large.
